INTERACTION ATLAS
A live social experiment: a shared, rule-minimal digital space in which people survive, connect, and continue only by being interesting to one another.
How it works:
• Draw or write anywhere on the canvas
• Zoom in/out to explore the infinite space
• Respond directly on top of others' work
• Erase or overwrite content freely
• All actions are transparent - participant numbers are visible
• Vote to remove participants if needed (4+ votes required)
• After 20-40 minutes, you'll be asked if you want to continue together
There are no predefined topics, no categories, no algorithms. What remains is human judgment.
